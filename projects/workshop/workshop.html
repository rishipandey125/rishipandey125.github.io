what is glyf?

glyf is a graphic design tool for creating spatial posters and sharing them with anyone via the web

ok so what is a spatial poster?

spatial posters are graphical elements in 3D space organized to tell a story or share information in spatial computing

spatial computing: human computer interaction in 3D space on traditional, AR, and VR devices

using glyf I turned this great article about NASA's JWST into a spatial poster - so I could explore it in AR like a little musuem exihibit!

 

here we are exploring this poster in AR via the Meta Quest Pro!

 

spatial posters are just the beginning, as we evolve the graphical components you can create with in glyf and bring interactivity to our scenes the medium can evolve into spatial websites for all: traditional computers and AR/VR devices!
